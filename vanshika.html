VANSHIKA'S PERSPECTIVE

A personal way of looking at reality, consciousness, free will, and the possibility that life itself may be a simulation.

My Personal Thought

I feel like we are all living inside a simulation. Not because someone proved it scientifically, but because many things in life make me question whether reality is exactly what it appears to be.

Every day people work, study, build careers, make decisions, fall in love, argue, succeed, and fail. Yet sometimes it feels as if people are not completely controlling their own actions. It feels as though something larger is influencing them, guiding them, or making them behave in specific ways.

This creates a powerful question: Are we truly making every decision ourselves, or are we following a script that we cannot fully see?

The Question Of Human Actions

Look carefully at human life. People wake up, follow routines, respond to emotions, follow social expectations, and often repeat the same patterns throughout life.

Many choices are influenced by family, society, education, fear, desire, culture, and circumstances.

Because of this, I sometimes wonder: How much of our life is truly our own choice, and how much is already being shaped by forces outside our awareness?

If reality is a simulation, then perhaps what we call free will is only part of a larger system.

Closing Our Eyes

Another thought often comes to my mind.

When humans close their eyes, they see darkness. The visible world disappears. They only see the world when they consciously choose to open their eyes and observe it.

This reminds me of a video game. A game only renders what the player is looking at. The world appears when attention is directed toward it.

Of course, science explains this through vision and brain activity. But philosophically, it still raises an interesting question: Does observation play a deeper role in reality than we currently understand?

Earth As A Giant Game

I sometimes imagine Earth as a giant game world.

Inside a game, characters live their lives. They walk through cities, experience victories and failures, interact with others, and believe their world is real.

If those characters suddenly became conscious, they would probably think: "This world is real because it is all I have ever known."

Humans may be similar. We experience reality as real because it is the only reality available to us.

Whether the universe is physical, spiritual, or simulated, our experiences still feel real.

Could Someone Be Operating Us?

Just as gamers control characters, I wonder whether some higher intelligence could be interacting with our reality.

Perhaps there is a system beyond our understanding. Perhaps there is a level of reality beyond this one. Or perhaps reality is exactly as science currently describes it.

The truth is that we do not know.

But asking questions is important. Because every major discovery in history began with someone questioning what everyone else accepted.

"Maybe reality is not fake. Maybe reality is simply deeper than we think."

— Vanshika's Perspective
